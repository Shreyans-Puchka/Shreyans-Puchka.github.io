Chocolate Ice Cream

Our rich chocolate ice cream is made with premium cocoa for a smooth, creamy texture and a deep chocolate flavour. It is a perfect treat for every chocolate lover.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 100 g dark chocolate, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Mix the sugar and cocoa powder in a bowl.
  2. Add the milk and stir until the cocoa and sugar dissolve completely.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream, melted dark chocolate, vanilla extract, and salt.
  4. Mix until the mixture is smooth and well combined.
  5. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker.
  6. Churn for 20–25 minutes, or according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  7. Transfer the ice cream to an airtight container.
  8. Freeze for 2–4 hours before serving.

Tips

  • Use good-quality cocoa powder for a richer flavour.
  • Chill the mixture before churning for a creamier texture.
  • Store in an airtight container in the freezer for up to 2 weeks.
  • Serve with chocolate chips, chocolate syrup, or brownies for an extra special dessert.
