Vanilla Ice Cream

Our classic vanilla ice cream is smooth, creamy, and made with simple, high-quality ingredients. It is perfect on its own or served with your favourite desserts.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix the heavy cream, whole milk, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt.
  2. Stir until the sugar has completely dissolved.
  3. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker.
  4. Churn for about 20–25 minutes, or according to the manufacturer's instructions.
  5. Transfer the ice cream to an airtight container.
  6. Freeze for 2–4 hours for a firmer texture.
  7. Scoop into bowls or cones and serve chilled.

Tips

  • Use pure vanilla extract for the best flavour.
  • Chill the mixture before churning for a creamier texture.
  • Store in an airtight container in the freezer for up to 2 weeks.
  • Serve with fresh fruits, chocolate sauce, or sprinkles if desired.
