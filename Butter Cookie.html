Classic Butter Cookies

A simple, crispy, and delicious homemade butter cookie recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Beat the butter and powdered sugar until light and creamy.
  3. Add the vanilla extract and mix well.
  4. Add the flour and salt, then mix into a soft dough.
  5. Roll the dough to about 5 mm thickness.
  6. Cut into your favourite cookie shapes.
  7. Place the cookies on a baking tray lined with baking paper.
  8. Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.
  9. Let the cookies cool completely before serving.

Tips

  • Use real butter for the best flavour.
  • Do not overmix the dough.
  • Store the cookies in an airtight container for up to one week.
